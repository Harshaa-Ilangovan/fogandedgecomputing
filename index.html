PROBLEM STATEMENT

HEALTH CARE

MANUFACTURING

Problem: Monitoring patients' vital signs in real-time and providing immediate medical assistance when necessary.

Solution: Wearable devices equipped with edge computing capabilities can continuously monitor patient data, detect anomalies, and provide timely alerts to healthcare professionals.

Problem: Manufacturing facilities need to optimize production processes, detect defects, and minimize downtime.

Solution: Edge devices on the factory floor can process sensor data to monitor machine health, predict maintenance needs, and improve overall efficiency.

CONCLUSION

Fog and edge computing are ushering in a new era of computing that empowers real-life applications with greater speed, reliability, security, and efficiency. As these technologies continue to evolve and mature, we can expect even more innovative use cases and a broader adoption across various industries, ultimately shaping the future of computing and connectivity.

  • Enhanced Latency-sensitive Applications
  • Bandwidth Optimization
  • Improved Privacy and Security
  • Reliability
  • Scalability
  • Cost Efficiency
